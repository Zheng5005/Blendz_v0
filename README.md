## What is the point of this project
The best way to learn a language is to immerse yourself in it—by meeting people who speak it. But there’s a small problem: you don’t know anyone who speaks your target language, and you don’t know how to get in touch with them.

This is a problem I know all too well. That’s why I created **Blendz**—a platform where you can connect with people for small talk and improve your target language. And hey, maybe you’ll even make a friend along the way :)
